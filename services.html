Our Core Services

At Focamark Surveys, we combine precision instrumentation with advanced geospatial analysis to provide actionable data for engineering, construction, and land management.

1

Land & Boundary Surveying

The foundation of any project starts with accurate boundaries. We provide legally defensible surveys to secure your property interests.

  • Perimeter & Topographic Surveys: Detailed mapping of physical features and elevations.
  • Cadastral Surveying: Precise boundary demarcation and land partitioning.
  • Title & Deed Plan Preparation: Essential documentation for land registration and legal transfers.
2

Engineering & Construction Surveys

We provide the high-tolerance measurements required for large-scale infrastructure and architectural projects.

  • Setting Out: Precise positioning of structural elements (piles, columns, roads) from design plans to the ground.
  • As-Built Surveys: Verifying that finished structures match original design specifications.
  • Monitoring Surveys: Tracking structural movements or ground subsidence over time.
3

Geospatial Analysis & GIS

Turn raw data into strategic insights. We help organizations manage spatial information for better decision-making.

  • GIS Mapping: Digital mapping solutions for urban planning and resource management.
  • Spatial Data Infrastructure (SDI): Organizing and managing large-scale geographical datasets.
  • Route Optimization: Specialized mapping for pipelines, cables, and road networks.
4

Hydrographic Surveying

Specialized underwater mapping for marine and coastal projects.

  • Bathymetric Surveys: Measuring the depth and terrain of sea, river, or lake beds.
  • Dredging Support: Calculating volumes and monitoring progress for maritime excavations.
  • Shoreline Mapping: Environmental monitoring and coastal erosion analysis.
5

Aerial Mapping & Remote Sensing

Utilizing the latest UAV (Drone) technology for rapid, high-resolution data acquisition.

  • Orthomosaic Mapping: High-resolution, corrected aerial imagery for site overviews.
  • LiDAR Scanning: Creating 3D models and terrain maps through dense vegetation or complex structures.
  • Volumetric Analysis: Fast and accurate stockpile measurements for mining and construction.
